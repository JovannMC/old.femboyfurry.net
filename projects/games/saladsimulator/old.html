This is the first game I ever released to the public. It is very scuffed and is based on Salad. This has some gameplay and definitely has more features than my other game, Salad Clicker.

You utilize Salad to earn money with your PC, and with that money, you use it to upgrade your PC to earn more money. It features a scuffed house, a computer, going outside (which breaks the game), and of course the Salad app itself. The computer has a half-broken GPU tuner and a web browser where you can visit Omazing:tm: to buy your hardware.

If you want to play the game, you can play it online through the main site, or download it.
