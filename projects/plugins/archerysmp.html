A free commission.

My first Minecraft plugin commission, which is a plugin for the ArcherySMP Minecraft server.

"A plugin for the ArcherySMP containing archers and hunters with a few abilities, tailored exactly to the requests.

Archers receive a powerful bow that shoots a "super arrow" when left clicking, and gives you "strength" when right clicking.
Hunters receive a powerful crossbow that poisons surrounding players when left clicking."

Tested versions: 1.20-1.20.1
Native version: 1.20
