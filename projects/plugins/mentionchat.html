This is the second plugin I released to the public, after JustFly. This was actually meant to be released years back in 2021, but has finally been finished and released.

"A simple and open source plugin that allows players to mention other players in chat, which is in basically every chat platform. You can configure the messages, mention type and sound, cooldown time, and more!"

Tested versions: 1.8 - 1.20.1
Native version: 1.13
